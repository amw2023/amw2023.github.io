Important Points

For each paper accepted for presentation at AMW 2023, there needs to be at least one author registered with the Chilean or International Regular fee. This includes papers co-authored by students.

Registration fees include:

  • AMW School May 22 - 23
  • AMW Workshop May 24 - 26
  • Coffee breaks everyday
  • Welcome Cocktail Wed May 23
  • Gala Dinner Thu May 24
Note that to register with STUDENT rate, you should provide contact details of your supervisor.

HOW TO REGISTER?

International Atendees
  1. Select the appropiated card-based payment option shown in this link.
  2. Follow the payment instruc;ons and save the voucher.
  3. Send an email to amw2023org@gmail.com with subject "AMW 2023 Registration". Please include your full name, affiliation, and country. If you are an author of a paper accepted to AMW 2023, add the title. If you are a student, please add the name and email of your supervisor. Optionally, add your arrival and departure dates, as well as any dietary restrictions or food allergies you have. Also attach the voucher of your payment.
